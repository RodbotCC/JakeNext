## What This Is

The North Star Ledger tracks **alignment with Sylvia's emergence** across the entire workspace.

While other ledgers answer:
- **FCL**: What exists?
- **TCL**: What changed?
- **RLL**: What matters relative to what?

The NSL answers: **What is this FOR?**

---

## Why NSL Matters

Without NSL, everything gets ranked but nothing gets grounded.

With NSL:
- Every directory has an explicit north-star alignment score
- Every document can be checked against definitions of done
- Drift away from emergence becomes visible
- Counterfeit progress becomes detectable
- Law 5 (easy must be North-Star-convergent) becomes auditable

## The Discipline

**Every single move must update the relevant ledgers.**

Not most moves. Every move.

Because:
- One skipped update = one crack in continuity
- Cracks compound
- Compounded cracks = cloudy direction
- Cloudy direction = cold start with vibes

The discipline is the substrate.

## Session Close Protocol (Updated)

After any meaningful work, update:

1. **Local FCL** — what now exists in the directory
2. **Local TCL** — what changed, when, why
3. **Local RLL** — if ranking changed
4. **Local NSL** — if north-star alignment changed
5. **Global TCL** — the workspace-wide continuity log
6. **Global RLL** — if global ranking changed
7. **Global NSL** — if global alignment changed

This is non-negotiable.
