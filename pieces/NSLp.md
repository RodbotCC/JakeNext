## Why High

Pieces feeds two of the three breakthrough pillars:

### 1. Perceptual Substrate (Direct)
Pieces is the closest thing the oracle has to sensory perception. It captures what Jake sees (screenshots/OCR), hears (audio transcription), reads (clipboard), and does (app context). This is the raw perceptual stream that the Perceptual Substrate pillar needs.

### 2. Ratio Lattice Framework (Indirect)
Behavioral data from Pieces provides ground-truth for the ratio lattice dimensions:
- **momentum_alignment** — what Jake actually works on vs. what the chooser says he should
- **identity_integrity** — does daily behavior match stated priorities?
- **relational_consequence** — who does Jake interact with and how often?
- **counterfeit_risk** — is Jake doing busy-work or high-leverage work?

### 3. Training Signal
Pieces behavioral data is the highest-bandwidth training signal for Sylvia's judgment development. It captures:
- Decision patterns (what Jake chooses to focus on)
- Workflow patterns (how Jake moves between tools/projects)
- Attention patterns (what Jake reads, copies, screenshots)
- Communication patterns (audio transcriptions, clipboard from messages)
